CCG Fan Club

The full name is "Commission of Counter Ghoul".
The organization established under the Ghoul Countermeasures Act is a gathering place for CCG Search Officer and has branches in various areas of Tokyo.
The main mission is to expel, sanction and research Ghoul, and also to accommodate orphans whose parents were killed by Ghoul.
After the battle between 24 Zone and V and the clowns, the CCG in Tokyo was disintegrated and reorganized into "TCG (Tokyo Security Committee)", half of whom were former CCG members.

Ghoul Countermeasures Act

Humans voluntarily expelled and sanctioned Ghoul to join the revised and amended laws. Since then, the countermeasures method has served as the highest standard for humans to deal with Ghoul problems.

Article 12, Item 1: "Once it is confirmed that the subject launched kakugan (red eyes) or kagune (RC skin), it is determined to be a special alert object of type I, otherwise known as" Ghoul "

Article 12, Item 2: "The individual who is judged to be" species "is not protected by any law"

Article 13, Item 1: "Ghoul search officers must put the safety of residents first"

Article 13, Item 2: "Severe injuries caused to" Ghoul "are prohibited"

Others: The act of hiding or hiding Ghoul will be severely punished to the same extent as other criminals who hide human beings.
